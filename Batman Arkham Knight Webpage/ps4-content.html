Exclusive PS4 Content

The Limited Edition set for PS4 will have Exclusive DLCS to play as different characters such as: Harley Quinn, Scarecrow, Robin and Nightwing. The set will come with the following items:

  • Custom Art Book:

    80 Page, Full Color Art Book Showcasing the concept art of Batman Arkham Knight.
  • Limited Edition SteelBook:

    SteelBook case and game disc.
  • Comic Book:

    Limited Edition DC Comics Batman: Arkham Knight #0 Comic Book.
  • Exclusive Character Skin Pack:

    Three Unique Skins from DC Comics, The New 52.
  • Batman Memorial Statue:

    A statue of Batman looking over Gotham.
  • The Limited Batmobile Edition:

    For $200 you can get all of the other items mentioned and a fully tranformable model of the Batmobile that also lights up.
